About the Director

Our goal is to learn the next generation of creative professionals for a future in any industry.

Devika is a seasoned HR Professional with extensive industrial experience. Holding an MBA in HR and Marketing and a PGDM in Talent Management from XLRI, she is also a certified wellness coach. She is certified by the Berkeley Institute of Wellbeing, California, on the science of happiness.She has extensive experience as a business communication trainer as well. Her journey as a happiness coach emerged as a response to the prevailing discontent and vexation in our busy lives.
